a useful assistant for using poe.trade

It automatically runs your searches every 5 mins (by default). This is very useful when waiting for an item(s) to go online, waiting for somebody to sell a Headhunter, or a 10c Tabula Rasa. It works by going through your list of poe.trade search urls and automatically grabs the search results for you. Then it shows you the "want to buy messages", as well as the item stats via tooltip. Simply clicking on one of the Want to buy messages will automatically copy the message for you into your clipboard. After that, just ctrl+v into the in-game chat window. I hope this will help improve your Path of Exile trading experience. Enjoy!

Note: You can also double click on the rows of Search List to edit.

  • save your searches for easier access
  • name your searches
  • useful for checking offline items if they're online now
  • a thousand times faster search speed
